Our Story

It all started on a rainy Tuesday in a small coffee shop. John, engrossed in his book, accidentally spilled his latte on Sarah's new scarf. What began as an apology quickly turned into laughter, then a long conversation, and eventually, a first date. From that moment, we knew something special was brewing.

Through countless adventures, shared dreams, and unwavering support, our love has grown stronger with each passing day. We've explored new cities, hiked breathtaking trails, and even survived assembling flat-pack furniture together. Every moment has been a testament to our bond.

Now, we're incredibly excited to embark on our greatest adventure yet: marriage. We can't wait to celebrate this new chapter with all of our beloved friends and family.
